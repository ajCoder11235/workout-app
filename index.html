Monday - Full Body Focus

Morning Warm-up (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Child's Pose (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Kneeling Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Jumping Jacks (25 seconds)
  • Plank (Hold for 25 seconds)
  • Light arm/leg swings

Workout

Perform 2 Sets of Each Exercise with 60-90 seconds rest between sets.

Upper Body

  • Arm Raises (15-20 reps) (Hand Weights)
  • Push Ups (7-9 reps - modify on knees)
  • Rhomboid Pulls (15-20 reps) (Resistance Band)

Lower Body

  • Butt Bridge (15-20 reps) (Band around thighs)
  • Side Lying Leg Lifts (12-15 reps/side) (Ankle Weights/Band)

Cool-down (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Cobra Stretch (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Pelvic Tilt (12-15 repetitions)
  • Bridge Hip Abduction (12-15 repetitions)
  • Flipping Thighs (20 seconds per side)
  • Up N Down (12-15 repetitions)

Tuesday - Upper Body & Core Focus

Morning Warm-up (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Child's Pose (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Frog Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Light arm circles (10-15 each direction)

Workout

Perform 2 Sets of Each Exercise with 60-90 seconds rest between sets.

Upper Body

  • Staggered Push Ups (5-7 reps/side)
  • Side Arm Raises (15-20 reps) (Hand Weights)
  • Diamond Push Ups (4-6 reps)

Lower Body

  • Lunges (8-10 reps/leg) (Hand/Ankle Weights)

Core

  • Abdominal Crunches (15-20 reps)

Cool-down (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Cobra Stretch (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Pelvic Tilt (12-15 repetitions)
  • Bridge Hip Abduction (12-15 repetitions)
  • Flipping Thighs (20 seconds per side)
  • Up N Down (12-15 repetitions)

Wednesday - Core & Lower Body Focus

Morning Warm-up (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Child's Pose (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Kneeling Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Knee to Chest Stretch (25 seconds per leg)
  • Light leg swings (10 each direction)

Workout

Perform 2 Sets of Each Exercise with 60-90 seconds rest between sets.

Core

  • Leg Raises (10-12 reps) (Ankle Weights)
  • Straight Arm Plank (Hold for 25 seconds)

Upper Body

  • Arm Scissors (20-25 reps) (Hand Weights)
  • Wide Arm Push Ups (5-7 reps)

Cool-down (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Cobra Stretch (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Frog Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Bridge Hip Abduction (12-15 repetitions)
  • Flipping Thighs (20 seconds per side)
  • Up N Down (12-15 repetitions)

Thursday - Lower Body & Core Focus

Morning Warm-up (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Child's Pose (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Pelvic Tilt (12-15 repetitions)
  • Marching in place (30-60 seconds)

Workout

Perform 2 Sets of Each Exercise with 60-90 seconds rest between sets.

Lower Body

  • Squats (10-12 reps) (Band/Weights)
  • Calf Raises (20-25 reps) (Hand Weights)

Core

  • V-Ups (8-10 reps) (Ankle Weights - Advanced)

Upper Body

  • Arm Circle Clockwise (25 seconds) (Hand Weights)
  • Inclined Push Ups (10-12 reps - use a counter)

Cool-down (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Cobra Stretch (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Kneeling Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Bridge Hip Abduction (12-15 repetitions)
  • Flipping Thighs (20 seconds per side)
  • Up N Down (12-15 repetitions)

Friday - Full Body Mix & Mobility

Morning Warm-up (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Child's Pose (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Frog Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Light dynamic stretches (leg swings, torso twists)

Workout

Perform 2 Sets of Each Exercise with 60-90 seconds rest between sets.

Upper Body

  • Tricep Dips (8-10 reps - use chair)
  • Knee Push Ups (10-12 reps)

Core

  • Arm Circle Anticlockwise (25 seconds) (Hand Weights)
  • Reclined Squeezes (15-20 reps)

Lower Body

  • Thrusting (Hip Thrusts) (15-20 reps) (Band around thighs)

Cool-down (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Cobra Stretch (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Pelvic Tilt (12-15 repetitions)
  • Kneeling Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Bridge Hip Abduction (12-15 repetitions)
  • Flipping Thighs (20 seconds per side)
  • Up N Down (12-15 repetitions)

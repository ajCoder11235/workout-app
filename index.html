Monday - Full Body Focus

Morning Warm-up (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Child's Pose (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Kneeling Hip Thrust (12-15 repetitions)
  • Jumping Jacks (25 seconds)
  • Plank (Hold for 25 seconds)
  • Light arm/leg swings

Workout

Perform 2 Sets of Each Exercise with 60-90 seconds rest between sets.

Upper Body

  • Arm Raises (15-20 reps) (Hand Weights)
  • Push Ups (7-9 reps - modify on knees)
  • Rhomboid Pulls (15-20 reps) (Resistance Band)

Lower Body

  • Butt Bridge (15-20 reps) (Band around thighs)
  • Side Lying Leg Lifts (12-15 reps/side) (Ankle Weights/Band)

Cool-down (approx. 5-7 minutes)

  • Cobra Stretch (Hold for 20-30 seconds)
  • Pelvic Tilt (12-15 repetitions)
  • Bridge Hip Abduction (12-15 repetitions)
  • Flipping Thighs (20 seconds per side)
  • Up N Down (12-15 repetitions)
